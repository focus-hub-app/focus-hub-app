About FocusHub 🎓

Empowering students to study smarter, not harder.

Our Mission

FocusHub was built with one goal in mind: to help students stay focused, organized, and motivated. Whether you're preparing for exams, learning a new subject, or just trying to build better study habits — FocusHub has the tools you need.

What We Offer

  • 🍅 Pomodoro Timer with streak tracking
  • 🧠 AI-powered quiz generator from your notes
  • 🃏 Smart flashcard system
  • 👥 Collaborative study rooms
  • 🏆 Leaderboards to keep you motivated
  • 📊 Daily study stats and streaks

Our Team

FocusHub is built by a passionate team of students and educators who believe that the right tools can make all the difference in academic success.

Free for Everyone

Our core features are completely free. We offer a Premium plan for users who want advanced AI features, ad-free experience, and private study groups.

Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

FocusHub collects minimal data to provide you with the best study experience. We may collect:

  • Usage data (time studied, sessions, quiz scores) stored locally in your browser
  • Contact information if you reach out to us
  • Anonymous analytics to improve our service

How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used solely to improve your experience on FocusHub. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

We use browser localStorage to save your study progress, streaks, and preferences. No cookies are used for tracking without consent.

Third-Party Services

FocusHub may use third-party services including Google AdSense and analytics tools. These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

FocusHub is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
